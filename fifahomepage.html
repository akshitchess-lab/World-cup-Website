Point Calculation Methodology

How your score is calculated.

Predictions are scored using a tiered accuracy system. Only the highest applicable tier is awarded per match.

🎯Scoring System
Points are awarded for each match prediction based on your accuracy. Only the highest applicable tier per match is awarded.
TierConditionPoints
🥇 Exact ScoreYou predicted the exact final score20 pts
🥈 Correct MarginThe winning margin matches15 pts
🥉 Correct ResultYou predicted Win, Loss, or Draw correctly10 pts
✕2'Double' Powerup Mechanics
You are allocated 5 'Double' powerups to use throughout the tournament. When applied to a match, your points for that match are multiplied by two.

❓Frequently Asked Questions
When are scores updated?
Official results are entered by the admin after each match. Standings update immediately.
Can I change a prediction?
Yes — scores auto-save as you type. Once an admin marks a match completed, results lock in.
What happens in a tied knockout match?
A penalty shootout predictor appears. The tie-breaker score determines who advances.
